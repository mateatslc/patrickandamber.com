Amber adopted Baxter when she was a young farm kitten. Together, they made many lonely trips to Petsmart, and Baxter eventually decided she wanted to start a family.

Knocked up at the age of one, she became a teen mom and gave birth to four adorable kittens. She raised them in Patrick’s apartment and decided to keep Jan as “the chosen one.”

Despite his disability (he cannot extend his back legs and walks like a rabbit), he loves to play outside and aspires to be an entomologist. His favorite movie is Toy Story and he refuses to drink water more than 30 seconds old.

Amber adopted 4-month-old Lucie on her 23rd birthday from the Humane Society of Utah, at a “Dog Days” game at Spring Mobile Ballpark. Lucie has learned every trick very quickly, but somehow “forgets” to obey her parents when she is outside or off leash.

She returned next year to “Dog Days” at Spring Mobile Ballpark to compete in an eating contest on top of the dugout, losing a very close match to a gigantic husky.

Patrick adopted 10-month-old Porter at another “Dog Days” event at Spring Mobile Ballpark approximately one month after Amber adopted Lucie. Porter is a very insistent guard dog, but in his spare time you can catch him chasing butterflies.

In addition, his height has bestowed him with the unique ability to sneak food off of the counter/table tops. You can catch both Porter and Lucie walking around the stadium during the summer, especially on fireworks nights.

Patrick was born in Colorado and grew up in Great Falls, Montana. He attended the University of Minnesota, where he took up competitive cycling. This interest traveled with him to Utah and culminated in his participation in Lotoja, a one-day, 208 mile bike race.

Currently, he is engaged in the seemingly endless pursuit of a Ph.D. in Neuroscience at the University of Utah (do not ask him how much time he has left in school).

Patrick enjoys hiking, camping, spending time with his pets, and exploring Utah. He has recently started filling his time with Clint Eastwood movies and cheap beer. In addition, he is excited about his recent and long-anticipated purchase of a hammock.

Amber was born in North Carolina and grew up in Concord, Ohio. After randomly picking a college in the west, she attended Utah State University and graduated in 2005.

She currently works as a “Research Scientist” in a vision research lab working on glaucoma and loves her job. Recently she spoke at a national conference called “ARVO” after being employed only eight months in her lab! One of the many wonderful things she enjoys about her job is working in the same cubicle as Patrick, literally one floor above.

Amber enjoys cycling, an event that lead her to participate in Lotoja, the same one day trek Patrick participated in. In her spare time, Amber enjoys reading on her nook, playing with her pets, exploring Utah, and listening to podcasts about random information. A future hobby Amber looks forward to pursuing is riding a tandem bike competitively with Patrick.
